LAKSHADWEEP

WE BRING LIFE,
TO YOUR DREAMS

The name Lakshadweep means "one lakh islands" in Malayalam and Sanskrit, though the islands are part of an archipelago of few islands. English designated as official language while Jeseri, a dialect of Malayalam is the widely spoken native language in the territory with Dhivehi being the most spoken in Minicoy island. The region comes under the judicial jurisdiction of the Kerala High Court. Fishing and agriculture are the major occupations in the islands
